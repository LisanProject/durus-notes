The Divine Attributes Chart

The twenty obligatory attributes (sifat wajibah), by category. For review, not first study; see the full notes for explanations and the rational proofs behind each.

This page prints cleanly (File › Print) as a compact multi-column handout.

1. Al-Nafsiyyah: The Attribute of Essence النفسية
Wujud الوجود

Existence. Stands alone in its own category: not one perfection alongside others, but the fact of being itself, without which no other attribute could be discussed.

2. Al-Salbiyyah: The Negating Attributes (5) السلبية
Each rules out one specific imperfection rather than asserting a positive quality on its own.
Qidam القدم

Pre-eternity. Rules out: a beginning.

Baqa' البقاء

Everlastingness. Rules out: an end.

Mukhalafah lil-Hawadith مخالفة للحوادث

Difference from creation. Rules out: resemblance to created things.

Qiyam bi-Nafsihi القيام بنفسه

Self-subsistence. Rules out: dependency on another for existence.

Wahdaniyyah الوحدانية

Oneness. Rules out: multiplicity, in essence, attributes, and acts.

3. Sifat al-Ma'ani: The Attributes of Meaning (7) صفات المعاني
Real, positive qualities genuinely subsisting in Allah's essence.
Hayah الحياة

Life. The precondition for every other ma'ani attribute; none of the rest are conceivable without it.

'Ilm العلم

Knowledge. Encompassing all that is knowable, without exception or increase.

Iradah الإرادة

Will. Specifies one possibility out of all that is merely possible.

Qudrah القدرة

Power. Bringing the willed possibility into actual existence.

Sam' السمع

Hearing. Encompassing all that can be heard, without organ or instrument.

Basar البصر

Sight. Encompassing all that can be seen, without organ or instrument.

Kalam الكلام

Speech. Eternal, not composed of letters or sounds; the Qur'an as recited is its created expression.

4. Al-Ma'nawiyyah: The Attributes Derived From Meaning (7) المعنوية
Not a fifth set of independent realities: each is simply the necessary state entailed by its ma'ani counterpart above.
Kawnuhu Hayyan كونه حيا

Being Living. Entailed by Hayah.

Kawnuhu 'Aliman كونه عالما

Being Knowing. Entailed by 'Ilm.

Kawnuhu Muridan كونه مريدا

Being Willing. Entailed by Iradah.

Kawnuhu Qadiran كونه قادرا

Being Powerful. Entailed by Qudrah.

Kawnuhu Sami'an كونه سميعا

Being Hearing. Entailed by Sam'.

Kawnuhu Basiran كونه بصيرا

Being Seeing. Entailed by Basar.

Kawnuhu Mutakalliman كونه متكلما

Being Speaking. Entailed by Kalam.

The Structure of the Forty-One
The Count
1 nafsiyyah + 5 salbiyyah + 7 ma'ani + 7 ma'nawiyyah= 20 wajib
Twenty logical opposites of the above= 20 mustahil
Doing or not doing a given contingent act= 1 ja'iz

Total: 41, said to be reflected in the 41 letters of لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّه.

This chart is a compressed reference; see the full notes on Why Twenty Attributes? for the rational proofs establishing each category and a qualified teacher for further explanation.
