Noon Sakinah (نْ) and Tanween
Idhhar Halqi الإظهار الحلقي

Pronounce noon/tanween clearly, no merging.

ء  ه  ع  ح  غ  خ

Default

Idgham الإدغام
With Ghunnahي ن م و
Without Ghunnahل ر

Exception (idhhar mutlaq, one word only): دنيا صنوان قنوان بنيان

Iqlab الإقلاب

Noon/tanween → hidden nasalized meem, before:

ب
Ikhfa' الإخفاء

Partial concealment, ghunnah retained, before the remaining 15 letters:

ت ث ج د ذ ز س ش ص ض ط ظ ف ق ك
Strongestت د ط
Weakestق ك
Intermediateremaining 10
Meem Sakinah (مْ)
Ikhfa' Shafawi الإخفاء الشفوي

Light nasal concealment before:

ب
Idgham Shafawi الإدغام الشفوي

Merges into a following meem: single elongated, nasalized meem.

م
Izhar Shafawi الإظهار الشفوي

Clear, before all other 26 letters. Caution: before waw and fa specifically, the meem is still clear, just easy to blur by accident.

Default

The Lam of Al and the Lam of the Verb
Lam Qamariyyah اللام القمرية

Clear (izhar). Mnemonic: ابغ حجك وخف عقيمه

ء ب غ ح ج ك و خ ف ع ق ي م ه
Lam Shamsiyyah اللام الشمسية

Assimilated, following letter doubled (shaddah). The other 14 letters not listed above.

ت ث د ذ ر ز س ش ص ض ط ظ ل ن
Lam of the Verb لام الفعل

Always izhar, no exceptions, regardless of the following letter.

e.g. قُلْ نَعَمْ, الْتَقَى

Letter Relationships
Mithlayn المتماثلان

Identical letters (same makhraj and sifat).

Mutajanisayn المتجانسان

Same makhraj, differing sifat.

Mutaqaribayn المتقاربان

Close makhraj, differing sifat.

Saghir / Kabir الصغير / الكبير
Saghir1st letter sakin → idgham
Kabirboth vowelled → izhar (default)
Madd: The Letters and Basic Types
Letters of Madd حروف المد
اalways after fatha
وsakin, after dammah
يsakin, after kasrah
Madd Tabi'i (Asli) المد الطبيعي

No hamzah or sukun follows. 2 harakat, fixed

Madd al-Leen مد اللين

Waw/ya sakin, preceded by fatha (not madd's own conditions). e.g. خَوْفٌ, بَيْتٌ

Madd Far'i: The Rulings
Muttasil المتصل

Hamzah after madd letter, same word.

Wajib 4-5 harakat

Munfasil المنفصل

Hamzah after madd letter, next word.

Jaiz 2, 4, or 5 harakat

'Arid li-l-Sukun العارض للسكون

Sukun only present at a pause (waqf).

Jaiz 2, 4, or 6 harakat

Badal البدل

Hamzah comes before the madd letter.

Jaiz usually 2 harakat

Lazim اللازم

Permanent sukun (wasl and waqf both).

Wajib/Lazim always 6 harakat

Madd Lazim: Four Types
Kilmiin an ordinary word
Harfiin the huruf muqatta'at
Muthaqqalidgham (shaddah) follows
Mukhaffafplain sukun, no idgham
Huruf Muqatta'at (Surah Openers)
Madd Lazim Harfi "كم عسل نقص"
ك م ع س ل ن ق ص

6 harakat ('ayn also permits 4-5)

Madd Tabi'i Harfi "حي طهر"
ح ي ط ه ر

2 harakat only

No Madd

Alif: never elongated when spelled out alone.
