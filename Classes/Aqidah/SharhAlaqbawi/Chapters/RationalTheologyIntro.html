Chapter 3: Rational Theology

This short chapter sets out the toolkit of rational laws classical Kalam uses to move from bare observation of the created world to Allah's core attributes, independently of scripture.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

The Three Rational Laws and the Proof of Life: Causality, congruity, and inseparability, and how these three laws are chained together to prove, in order, Allah's existence, power, will, knowledge, and life.
