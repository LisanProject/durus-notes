Chapter 2: The Negating Attributes

This chapter covers the five negating attributes (al sifat al salbiyyah): the qualities that rule out a specific imperfection rather than asserting a positive quality on their own. Each section pairs the relevant line of the matn with the rational proofs behind it.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

Existence, Pre Eternity, and Everlastingness: Wujud, Qidam, and Baqa': what each means precisely, and how they are proven through the law of causality and the law of change.

Unlike Creation, Dependent on Nothing: Mukhalafah lil Hawadith and Qiyam bi Nafsihi: why Allah cannot be a substance or an accident, and what total self sufficiency actually means.

Divine Oneness in Essence, Attributes, and Acts: Wahdaniyyah worked through in full: why a composite god is impossible, how Ash'ari occasionalism explains cause and effect, and how human moral responsibility survives it.
