Chapter 5: Certainty and Objections

This chapter steps back from the attributes themselves to ask a different question. What counts as sufficiently certain evidence for a matter of creed, and how the Ash'ari position is most commonly misread by its critics, are both covered here.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

Certainty, the Ahad Hadith, and Common Misconceptions covers why creed requires certainty, how a single narrator hadith can or cannot supply it, and a direct look at the most common misreadings of the Ash'ari position.
