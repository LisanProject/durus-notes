Assignment problems for Ash'ari Aqidah. These mirror the Practice Problems in topic and difficulty but without solutions shown, meant for self-testing or assigning to someone else. Full worked solutions for the same topics are on the Practice Problems page.

Sharh al-Aqbawi (Aqidah Tawhidiyyah)

  • The Three Rational Laws - Identify which rational law, Causality, Congruity, or Inseparability, a given argument or objection is applying.
  • Categorizing the Twenty Attributes - Identify which of the four categories, Essence, Negating, Meaning, or Derived from Meaning, a given attribute belongs to.
