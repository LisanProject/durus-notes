Liban's Online Notes

Al-Laqani

Notes in preparation. A poem of approximately 144 lines memorized in traditional curricula.

Topics

  1. Necessary Attributes - Extended Treatment - More detailed argumentation than in the Sanusiyyah; distinctions between the attributes.
  2. Prophethood (Nubuwwat) - The prophets, their number, the conditions of prophethood, and the question of miracles.
  3. Eschatology (Sam'iyyat) - What is known only through revelation: the Day of Judgment, the scales, the bridge, paradise and hellfire.
