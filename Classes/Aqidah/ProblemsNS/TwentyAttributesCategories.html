Categorizing the Twenty Attributes

For each attribute below, identify which of the four categories it belongs to: the Attribute of Essence (nafsiyyah), the Negating Attributes (salbiyyah), the Attributes of Meaning (ma'ani), or the Attributes Derived from Meaning (ma'nawiyyah).

  1. الْوُجُود (Existence)
  2. الْبَقَاء (Everlastingness)
  3. الْقُدْرَة (Power)
  4. كَوْنُهُ تَعَالَى قَادِرًا (His being Powerful)
  5. مُخَالَفَتُهُ لِلْحَوَادِث (Dissimilarity to Created Things)
