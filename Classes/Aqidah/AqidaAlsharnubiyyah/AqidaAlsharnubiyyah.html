Aqida al-Sharnubiyyah

A short Ash'ari creed poem attributed to Shaykh al-Sharnubi, commonly studied alongside or shortly after Umm al-Barahin. It restates the same core set of necessary, impossible, and permissible attributes in verse form, which makes it a useful memorization piece once the underlying concepts are already understood in prose.
