3. الْقُدْرَة (Power)

The Attributes of Meaning (sifat al-ma'ani). Qudrah is one of the seven real, subsisting attributes, alongside will, knowledge, life, hearing, sight, and speech, that positively describe Allah rather than merely ruling something out.
