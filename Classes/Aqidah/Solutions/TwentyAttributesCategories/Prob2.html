2. الْبَقَاء (Everlastingness)

The Negating Attributes (al-salbiyyah). Baqa' negates the possibility of Allah ceasing to exist, alongside pre-eternity (qidam), dissimilarity to creation (mukhalafah lil-hawadith), self-subsistence (qiyam bi-nafsihi), and oneness (wahdaniyyah), five attributes in total that each rule something out rather than affirming a positive quality directly.
