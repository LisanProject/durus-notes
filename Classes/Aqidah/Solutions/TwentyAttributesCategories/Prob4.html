4. كَوْنُهُ تَعَالَى قَادِرًا (His being Powerful)

The Attributes Derived from Meaning (al-ma'nawiyyah). This is not a fifth independent reality alongside qudrah itself; it is simply the state of actually being described by that attribute, "His being Powerful" as opposed to "Power" as a subsisting reality. Each of the seven ma'nawiyyah attributes tracks one of the seven ma'ani attributes this same way.
