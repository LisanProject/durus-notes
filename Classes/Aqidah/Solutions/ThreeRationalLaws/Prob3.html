3. Explain why a chain of dominoes falling cannot be used as a complete analogy for how Allah brings about change in the universe.

A domino chain illustrates the Law of Causality loosely, each fall causes the next, but it breaks down once you press on it: eventually you reach a first domino, and asking what caused that one either regresses infinitely, which the Law of Causality itself rules out (an infinite regress of caused causes would mean nothing is ever actually caused at all), or terminates in an uncaused cause, which is exactly the Maker the argument is building toward. The analogy is useful for the basic idea of cause and effect, but it cannot stand in for the full argument, since a chain of caused causes can never terminate the regress on its own.
