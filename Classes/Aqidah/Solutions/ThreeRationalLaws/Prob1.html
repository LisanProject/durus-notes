1. A student argues that the universe's order and precision are themselves evidence that it was brought about deliberately, since order of that kind does not arise from nothing.

This is the Law of Causality: every effect or change demands an external cause. The order and precision observed in the universe is a change or effect that cannot account for itself; something had to bring it about. This is the starting point for inferring a Maker (mujid), before any further reasoning about that Maker's attributes.
