Al-Dardir

Notes in preparation. A poem of 66 lines covering the Ash'ari creed - target: full memorization.

Topics

  1. Lines 1-20 - Necessary Attributes of Allah - Wujud, qidam, baqa', mukhalafah, qiyam bi-l-nafs, wahdaniyyah.
  2. Lines 21-45 - Attributes of Meaning & Meaningful States - Qudrah, iradah, ilm, hayah, sam', basar, kalam.
  3. Lines 46-66 - The Prophets & Conclusion - Attributes of the prophets and obligations upon the believer.
