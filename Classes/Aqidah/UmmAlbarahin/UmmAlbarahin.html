Liban's Online Notes

Al-Sanusi

Notes in preparation. One of the most widely memorized and taught creedal texts in the Ash'ari tradition.

Topics

  1. Introduction - Obligation of Knowledge - The first obligation on the mukallaf; the three categories of rational judgment.
  2. Wajib, Mustahil, Ja'iz for Allah - The twenty necessary, twenty impossible, and one possible attribute.
  3. Wajib, Mustahil, Ja'iz for the Prophets - The four necessary, four impossible, and one possible attribute of messengers.
  4. The Rational Proofs (Dala'il Aqliyyah) - Proof for each necessary attribute and refutation of its opposite.
