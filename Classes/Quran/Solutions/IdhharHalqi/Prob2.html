2. مِنْ خَوْفٍ

"from fear." The noon sakinah in مِنْ is immediately followed by kha in خَوْفٍ. Kha is the sixth and final throat letter, so idhhar applies: the noon is pronounced clearly and fully, exactly as it is spelled.
