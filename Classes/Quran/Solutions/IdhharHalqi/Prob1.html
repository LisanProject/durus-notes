1. عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ

"A painful punishment." The tanween at the end of عَذَابٌ is immediately followed by hamzah at the start of أَلِيمٌ. Hamzah is the first of the six throat letters, so idhhar applies: the tanween's added n sound is pronounced clearly and separately, with no merging into the hamzah.
