2. غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

"Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful." The tanween at the end of غَفُورٌ is followed by ra in رَّحِيمٌ. Ra belongs to the idgham-without-ghunnah group, so the tanween merges completely into the ra with no nasal trace, producing a doubled ra sound.
