3. قِنْوَانٌ

"clusters (of dates)." The noon sakinah and the waw both occur inside the single word قِنْوَانٌ, which is one of the four words in the Qur'an where idgham is blocked by the single-word exception. Idhhar mutlaq applies instead: the noon is pronounced clearly, with no merging into the waw, even though waw is normally an idgham letter.
