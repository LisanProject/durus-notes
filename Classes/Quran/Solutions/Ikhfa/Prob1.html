1. أَنفُسَكُمْ

"yourselves." The noon sakinah in أَنْ is followed by fa in فُسَكُمْ. Fa is one of the fifteen ikhfa' letters, and it falls in the intermediate group (neither the three closest letters nor the two farthest from noon's point), so the ikhfa' here is of moderate strength.
