3. مَاءً ثَجَّاجًا

"water pouring abundantly" (Surah an-Naba, 78:14). The tanween at the end of مَاءً is followed by tha in ثَجَّاجًا. Tha falls in the intermediate group of ikhfa' letters, neither the strongest nor the weakest, so a moderate degree of nasalization applies.
