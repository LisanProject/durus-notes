1. تَرْمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍ

"striking them with stones"

The meem sakinah in تَرْمِيهِمْ is immediately followed by ba in بِحِجَارَةٍ. Ikhfa' shafawi applies: the meem is concealed with a light nasal sound.
