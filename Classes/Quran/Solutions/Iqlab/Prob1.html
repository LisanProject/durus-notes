1. أَنبَتْنَا

"We caused to grow." The noon sakinah in أَنْ is immediately followed by ba in بَتْنَا, within the same word. Iqlab applies regardless of whether the noon and ba are in one word or two: the noon sound is converted into a light, nasalized meem before the ba is pronounced.
