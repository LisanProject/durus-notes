2. عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ

"Knowing of what is in the breasts." The tanween at the end of عَلِيمٌ is immediately followed by ba in بِذَاتِ. Iqlab applies to tanween exactly as it does to a written noon sakinah: the added n sound converts into a nasalized meem before the ba.
