Ibn al-Jazari (d. 833 AH)

The authoritative poem on Tajwid comprising 107 lines. Memorization is a prerequisite for serious study of the Qira'at. More technically precise than the Tuhfah.

Topics

  1. Lines 1-20 - Makharij al-Huruf - The points of articulation for every letter of the Arabic alphabet.
  2. Lines 21-50 - Sifat al-Huruf - The characteristics of each letter: jahr/hams, shiddah/rikhwah, itbaq/infitah, and others.
  3. Lines 51-80 - Rules of Nun, Mim & Madd - A more technical treatment than the Tuhfah, with additional distinctions.
  4. Lines 81-107 - Waqf, Ibtida' & Conclusion - Rules of stopping and starting; the famous closing passage on the obligation of tajwid.
