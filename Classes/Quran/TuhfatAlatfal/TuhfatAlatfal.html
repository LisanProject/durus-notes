Sulayman al-Jamzuri

A 61-line poem covering the foundational rules of Tajwid. The standard entry point into formal Tajwid study worldwide. Target: full memorization before moving to the Jazariyyah.

Topics

  1. Lines 1-10 - Introduction & Nun Sakinah - The four rules for the nun sakinah and tanwin: izhar, idgham, iqlab, ikhfa'.
  2. Lines 11-20 - Mim Sakinah & Shaddah - Ikhfa' shafawi, idgham shafawi, izhar shafawi.
  3. Lines 21-40 - Madd (Prolongation) - Types of madd: tabi'i, muttasil, munfasil, arid, and their lengths.
  4. Lines 41-61 - Lam, Ra' & Conclusion - Rules of the lam and ra'; tafkhim and tarqiq (heaviness and lightness).
