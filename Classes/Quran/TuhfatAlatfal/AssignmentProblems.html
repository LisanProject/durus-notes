Tuhfat al-Atfal - Assignment Problems

Here are the assignment problems for Tuhfat al-Atfal. These mirror the Practice Problems in topic and difficulty but without solutions shown.

Please do not email asking for solutions to these problems. Full worked solutions for the matching topics are on the Practice Problems page.

Chapter 1: Introduction

The Opening of the Poem: problems not yet posted

Chapter 2: Rules of the Noon Sakinah and Tanween

Idhhar Halqi: Identify the noon sakinah or tanween and the throat letter that follows it.

Idgham: Identify the noon sakinah or tanween, the letter that follows it, and which type of idgham applies (or whether the exception blocks it).

Iqlab: Identify the noon sakinah or tanween that converts to a nasalized meem before ba.

Ikhfa': Identify the noon sakinah or tanween, the letter that follows it, and which degree of ikhfa' applies.

Chapter 3: Rules of the Meem Sakinah

The Meem Sakinah: Identify the meem sakinah, the letter that follows it, and which of the three rulings applies.

Chapter 4: The Lam of Al and the Lam of the Verb

Lam Qamariyyah, Lam Shamsiyyah, and the Lam of the Verb: Identify a lam and state which rule applies.

Chapter 5: Mithlayn, Mutajanisayn, and Mutaqaribayn

The Three Letter Relationships: Classify a pair of adjacent letters and determine whether the pair is saghir or kabir.

Chapter 6: The Types of Madd

Madd Asli and Madd al-Leen: Distinguish a true letter of madd from a letter of leen.

Chapter 7: The Rulings of the Madd

Madd Wajib, Jaiz, and Lazim: Identify a letter of madd, its cause, and the ruling that governs it.

Chapter 8: The Types of Madd Lazim

Kilmi and Harfi, Mukhaffaf and Muthaqqal: Classify a madd lazim by its four-way type.
