Chapter 9: Conclusion of the Poem

With every ruling explained, al-Jamzuri closes the poem the way he opened it: with praise of Allah, followed by salutations upon the Prophet, peace be upon him, his family, his companions, and every reciter and listener of the Qur'an who comes after them. He also encodes two pieces of information into the closing lines using the abjad numeral system, a classical device for hiding a number inside ordinary-looking words.
