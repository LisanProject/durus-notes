Chapter 6: The Types of Madd

Madd, elongation, is one of the largest topics in Tuhfat al-Atfal, spanning this chapter and the next two. Madd literally means to lengthen or pull, and technically refers to prolonging the sound of a letter of madd or a letter of leen beyond its ordinary duration. This chapter lays the foundation: what the two basic categories of madd are, what the three letters of madd actually are and what conditions they require, and the related but distinct category of madd al-leen.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

Madd Asli (Tabi'i): The natural, two-count madd that requires no external cause, and why it is called "natural" in the first place.

The Letters of Madd and Madd al-Leen: The three letters of madd and their strict vowel conditions, plus the related but separate category of madd al-leen.
