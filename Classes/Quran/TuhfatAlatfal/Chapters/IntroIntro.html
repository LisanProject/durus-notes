Chapter 1: Introduction

This chapter covers the opening of the poem: the author's own introduction of himself and his teacher, the reason he composed the work, and the title he gave it. None of this is decoration. Classical texts open this way for specific reasons, and understanding why al-Jamzuri opens the way he does is itself part of learning to read a matn correctly.
