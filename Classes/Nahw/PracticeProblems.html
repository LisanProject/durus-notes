Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) - Practice Problems

Here are a set of practice problems for the Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) notes.

  1. If you'd like to view the solutions on the web, go to the section's problem list page and click the "Solution" link for any problem and it will take you to the solution for that problem.
  2. If you are looking for problems without solutions to test yourself cold, see the Assignment Problems page instead.

Note that some sections will have more problems than others, and problems will only appear once the corresponding notes section has been posted. Sections without problems yet are marked below.

Here is a list of all the sections for which practice problems have been written as well as a brief description of the material covered in the notes for that particular section.

Al-Ajrumiyyah

Word Types (Ism, Fi'l, Harf) – Here we practice identifying a word as ism, fi'l, or harf and stating the grammatical reason, drawing on the definitions from the Al-Kalam chapter.

Signs of the Ism – Here we identify the sign (khafd, tanwin, the definite article, a preposition, or a letter of oath) that shows a given word is a noun.

Signs of the Fi'l – Here we identify the sign (qad, the sin, sawfa, or the silent feminine ta) that shows a given word is a verb.

Signs of Rafa – Here we identify the sign of rafa on a given word and name the category (singular noun, plural, verb, and so on) it belongs to.

Signs of Nasb – Here we identify the sign of nasb on a given word, state whether it is explicit or implicit, and explain why, with several problems drawn directly from the Quran.
