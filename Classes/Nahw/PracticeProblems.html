Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) - Practice Problems

Here are the practice problems for the Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) notes, organized by book. Click on a book below to see the practice problems written for it so far.

If you are looking for problems without solutions to test yourself cold, see the Assignment Problems page instead.

Al-Ajrumiyyah – Ibn Ajurrum's foundational primer on Arabic grammar, covering the basic word categories, the signs of i’rab, and the core cases (rafa and nasb).

Qatr al-Nada wa Ball al-Sada – Ibn Hisham's intermediate text expanding on i’rab in detail, the complete marfu'at and mansubat, and the grammatical effects of particles.

Qawa'id al-I'rab – A focused text on the principles of i’rab and how particles are parsed, with dedicated Qur'anic i’rab exercises.

Alfiyyat Ibn Malik – Ibn Malik's advanced didactic poem covering the whole of Arabic grammar in verse form.
