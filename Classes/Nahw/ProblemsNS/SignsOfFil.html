Signs of the Fi'l

Please do not email asking for solutions to these problems. Full worked solutions for these problems are on the Practice Problems page for this section.

For each problem below, identify the sign that shows the underlined word is a verb.

  1. قَدْ حَفِظَ الْوَلَدُ الدَّرْسَ
  2. قَدْ يَصْدُقُ الْمُخْطِئُ أَحْيَانًا
  3. سَتُشْرِقُ الشَّمْسُ قَرِيبًا
  4. سَوْفَ يُسَافِرُ أَخِي غَدًا
  5. سَافَرَتْ فَاطِمَةُ إِلَى مَكَّةَ
