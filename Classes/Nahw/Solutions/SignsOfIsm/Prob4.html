4. ذَهَبْتُ إِلَى الْمَسْجِدِ

"I went to the mosque." الْمَسْجِدِ is a noun, identified twice over: by the preposition إِلَى (one of the huruf al-khafd) entering upon it, and by carrying the definite article ال.
