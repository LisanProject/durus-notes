7. Give four examples of a mansub singular noun: one masculine with explicit fatha, one masculine with implicit fatha, one feminine with explicit fatha, and one feminine with implicit fatha.

Masculine, explicit: لَقِيتُ عَلِيًّا. Masculine, implicit: لَقِيتُ الْفَتَى. Feminine, explicit: قَابَلْتُ هِنْدًا. Feminine, implicit: وَدَّعْتُ لَيْلَى.
