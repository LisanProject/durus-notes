2. لَمْ يَدْعُ مُحَمَّدٌ إِلاَّ إِلَى الْحَقِّ

The base verb is يَدْعُو, mu'tall al-akhir, ending in the weak letter و. Due to the particle of jazm لَمْ entering upon it, it is majzum by hadhf: the و is dropped entirely, giving يَدْعُ, rather than taking sukun the way a sahih al-akhir verb would.
