3. لَمْ يَضْرِبُوا

The base verb is يَضْرِبُونَ, one of the Five Verbs, whose sign of rafa is the retained nun. Due to the particle of jazm لَمْ entering upon it, it is majzum by hadhf al-nun: the نَ is dropped, giving يَضْرِبُوا, with the alif remaining as the fa'il, non-inflectable, standing in place of raf'.
