1. عَلِيٌّ لَمْ يَلْعَبْ

يَلْعَبْ is majzum due to the particle of jazm لَمْ entering upon it. The sign of it being majzum is the sukun, since it is a mudari' verb with a sound ending (sahih al-akhir).
