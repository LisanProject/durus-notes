Al-Ajrumiyyah - Practice Problems

Here are the practice problems for Al-Ajrumiyyah. Click on the "Solution" link for each problem to see the full worked solution. If you are looking for problems without solutions to test yourself cold, see the Assignment Problems page instead.

Note that some sections will have more problems than others, and problems will only appear once the corresponding notes section has been posted. Sections without problems yet are marked below.

Here is a listing of sections for which practice problems have been written, along with a brief description of what each section tests.

Chapter 1: Al-Kalam

Al-Kalam (the Basmala) has no problems and solutions posted yet.

Word Types (Ism, Fi'l, Harf) practices identifying a word as ism, fi'l, or harf and stating the grammatical reason, drawing on the definitions from the Al-Kalam chapter.

Signs of the Ism identifies the sign (khafd, tanwin, the definite article, a preposition, or a letter of oath) that shows a given word is a noun.

Signs of the Fi'l identifies the sign (qad, the sin, sawfa, or the silent feminine ta) that shows a given word is a verb.

Chapter 2: Al-I'rab

The Eight Word Categories has no problems and solutions posted yet.

Signs of Rafa identifies the sign of rafa on a given word and names the category (singular noun, plural, verb, and so on) it belongs to.

Signs of Nasb identifies the sign of nasb on a given word, states whether it is explicit or implicit, and explains why, with several problems drawn directly from the Quran.

Signs of Khafd identifies the sign of khafd on a given word and names the category (singular noun, one of the five nouns, a diptote, and so on) it belongs to.

Signs of Jazm identifies the sign of jazm on a given verb and names the category (sound ending, weak ending, or one of the Five Verbs) it belongs to.

More sections will be added here as later chapters of Al-Ajrumiyyah are posted.
