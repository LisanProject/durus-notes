Signs of Jazm علامات الجزم

Text
وَلِلْجَزْمِ عَلَامَتَانِ: السُّكُونُ، وَالْحَذْفُ. فَأَمَّا السُّكُونُ، فَيَكُونُ عَلَامَةً لِلْجَزْمِ فِي الْفِعْلِ الْمُضَارِعِ الصَّحِيحِ الْآخِرِ. وَأَمَّا الْحَذْفُ، فَيَكُونُ عَلَامَةً لِلْجَزْمِ فِي الْفِعْلِ الْمُضَارِعِ الْمُعْتَلِّ الْآخِرِ، وَفِي الْأَفْعَالِ الْخَمْسَةِ الَّتِي رَفْعُهَا بِثَبَاتِ النُّونِ.
Vocabulary
  • الْجَزْمjazm, the jussive mood, used for the present tense verb after a jazm particle
  • السُّكُونsukun, the absence of any vowel on the final letter
  • الْحَذْفhadhf, dropping a letter entirely as the sign of a case
  • صَحِيح الْآخِرsahih al-akhir, a verb whose final root letter is a regular consonant, not a weak letter
  • مُعْتَلّ الْآخِرmu'tall al-akhir, a verb whose final root letter is a weak letter (waw, ya, or alif)
  • الْأَفْعَال الْخَمْسَةal-af'al al-khamsa, the Five Verbs, present tense verbs attached to the dual alif, the plural waw, or the feminine singular ya
Translation
Jazm has two signs: the sukun and the hadhf. The sukun is a sign of jazm in the present tense verb with a sound final letter. The hadhf is a sign of jazm in the present tense verb with a weak final letter, and in the Five Verbs, whose sign of rafa is the retained nun.
Commentary

Jazm only ever applies to the present tense verb (al-fi'l al-mudari'), never to nouns. It is triggered by a jazm particle such as لَمْ, and unlike the other three cases, it has only two signs rather than three or more.

1. Al-Sukun marks jazm on a sahih al-akhir present tense verb, one whose final root letter is a regular consonant capable of carrying a vowel. Removing that vowel entirely, leaving the letter bare, is what jazm looks like for this category.

(1)

2. Al-Hadhf marks jazm in two separate categories. First, the mu'tall al-akhir present tense verb, whose final root letter is a weak letter (waw, ya, or alif), takes jazm by dropping that final weak letter entirely, since a weak letter cannot take a sukun the way a regular consonant can. Second, the Five Verbs, which take rafa by the retained nun, take jazm and nasb alike by dropping that same nun.

(2)
