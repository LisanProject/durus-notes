Al-Ajrumiyyah - Assignment Problems

Here are the assignment problems for Al-Ajrumiyyah. These mirror the Practice Problems in topic and difficulty but without solutions shown.

Please do not email asking for solutions to these problems. Full worked solutions for the matching topics are on the Practice Problems page.

Chapter 1: Al-Kalam

Al-Kalam (the Basmala) – problems not yet posted

Word Types (Ism, Fi'l, Harf) – Identify each word as ism, fi'l, or harf and give the grammatical reason.

Signs of the Ism – Identify the sign that shows a given word is a noun.

Signs of the Fi'l – Identify the sign that shows a given word is a verb.
