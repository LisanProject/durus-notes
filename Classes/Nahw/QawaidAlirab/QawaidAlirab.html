Liban's Online Notes

Ibn Hisham al-Ansari

A focused text on the rules governing grammatical parsing. I'rab practice drawn entirely from the Qur'an.

Topics

  1. Principles of I'rab - The methodology of parsing: identifying the grammatical position and its evidence.
  2. Particles & Their Parsing - How to parse every major particle; mahall i'rab for particle-governed clauses.
  3. Qur'anic I'rab Exercises - Worked parsing of selected ayat. Applied exercises for each rule.
