Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) - Assignment Problems

Here are the assignment problems for the Arabic Sciences (Nahw and Sarf) notes, organized by book. These mirror the Practice Problems in topic and difficulty but without solutions shown.

Please do not email asking for solutions to these problems. Full worked solutions for the matching topics are on the Practice Problems page.

Al-Ajrumiyyah covers Ibn Ajurrum's foundational primer on Arabic grammar, the basic word categories, the signs of i’rab, and the core cases (rafa and nasb).

Qatr al-Nada wa Ball al-Sada covers Ibn Hisham's intermediate text expanding on i’rab in detail, the complete marfu'at and mansubat, and the grammatical effects of particles.

Qawa'id al-I'rab covers the principles of i’rab and how particles are parsed, with dedicated Qur'anic i’rab exercises.

Alfiyyat Ibn Malik covers Ibn Malik's advanced didactic poem, the whole of Arabic grammar in verse form.
