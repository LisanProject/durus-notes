6. Writing: write a short paragraph, two to three sentences, comparing traveling by plane versus traveling by train, saying which you prefer and why.

أُفَضِّلُ السَّفَرَ بِالقِطَارِ عَلَى السَّفَرِ بِالطَّائِرَةِ، لِأَنَّ القِطَارَ أَرْخَصُ وَأَرَى المَنَاظِرَ الطَّبِيعِيَّةَ فِي الطَّرِيقِ. لَكِنَّ الطَّائِرَةَ أَسْرَعُ بِكَثِيرٍ.
I prefer traveling by train over traveling by plane, because the train is cheaper and I see the landscape along the way. But the plane is much faster.
