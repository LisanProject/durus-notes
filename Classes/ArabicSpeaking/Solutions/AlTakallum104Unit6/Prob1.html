Back to Problem List

1. Speaking: answer in a full sentence. أَيْنَ سَتَقْضِي عُطْلَتَكَ القَادِمَةَ؟ (Where will you spend your next vacation?)

سَأَقْضِي عُطْلَتِي القَادِمَةَ فِي الإِسْكَنْدَرِيَّةِ، بِجَانِبِ البَحْرِ.
I will spend my next vacation in Alexandria, by the sea. Any answer using a form of يَقْضِي plus a place is correct.
