4. Conjugate يَسْتَرِيحُ (he rests) for نَحْنُ in the present and past, then use the past-tense form in a sentence about a vacation.

Present: نَسْتَرِيحُ — Past: اسْتَرَحْنَا
Sentence: اسْتَرَحْنَا عَلَى شَاطِئِ البَحْرِ طُولَ الأُسْبُوعِ. (We rested on the beach all week.)
