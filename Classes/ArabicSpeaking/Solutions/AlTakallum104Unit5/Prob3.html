3. Role-play (speaking, out loud): you are at the currency exchange office. Ask the employee to change 100 US dollars into Egyptian pounds, then ask for today's rate.

أُرِيدُ أَنْ أُغَيِّرَ مِائَةَ دُولَارٍ أَمْرِيكِيٍّ إِلَى جُنَيْهٍ مِصْرِيٍّ. مَا سِعْرُ الدُّولَارِ اليَوْمَ؟
I want to exchange one hundred US dollars into Egyptian pounds. What is today's dollar rate?
