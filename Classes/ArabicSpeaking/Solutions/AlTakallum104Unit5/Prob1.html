1. Say the following time out loud in Arabic: 3:45. (اُنْطُقِ السَّاعَةَ التَّالِيَةَ)

السَّاعَةُ الرَّابِعَةُ إِلَّا رُبْعًا.
Literally “the fourth hour minus a quarter,” the standard construction for quarter to the hour.
