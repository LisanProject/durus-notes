4. Conjugate يَذْهَبُ for أَنْتِ in both the present and the past tense, then use the past-tense form in one full sentence about yesterday.

Present: تَذْهَبِينَ — Past: ذَهَبْتِ
Sentence: ذَهَبْتِ إِلَى المَطَارِ أَمْسِ. (You went to the airport yesterday.)
