104 Unit 5 — Times and Prices

Back to Problem List

5. Writing: write three sentences describing your daily schedule, each using a different time expression from Unit 5 (e.g. وَالنِّصْفُ, وَالرُّبْعُ, إِلَّا رُبْعًا).

أَسْتَيْقِظُ السَّاعَةَ السَّادِسَةَ وَالنِّصْفَ صَبَاحًا. أَذْهَبُ إِلَى العَمَلِ السَّاعَةَ الثَّامِنَةَ إِلَّا رُبْعًا. أَعُودُ إِلَى البَيْتِ السَّاعَةَ الخَامِسَةَ وَالرُّبْعَ مَسَاءً.
I wake up at six thirty in the morning. I go to work at quarter to eight. I return home at quarter past five in the evening. Any three sentences that correctly use three different time patterns are acceptable.
