6. Writing: write a short four-line dialogue between a customer and a bank employee exchanging Kuwaiti dinars for Egyptian pounds, including a question about the change.

الزَّبُونُ: أُرِيدُ أَنْ أُغَيِّرَ خَمْسِينَ دِينَارًا كُوَيْتِيًّا.
المُوَظَّفُ: بِالتَّأْكِيدِ، تَفَضَّلْ. هَذَا سِعْرُ الدِّينَارِ اليَوْمَ.
الزَّبُونُ: شُكْرًا. وَمَا البَاقِي؟
المُوَظَّفُ: البَاقِي عِشْرُونَ جُنَيْهًا.

Customer: I want to exchange fifty Kuwaiti dinars. Employee: Certainly, here you go, this is today's dinar rate. Customer: Thanks, and what's the change? Employee: The change is twenty pounds.
