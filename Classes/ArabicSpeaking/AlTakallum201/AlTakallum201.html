Speaking Practice

Unit 1 — Travel via Cairo

أَيْنَ تُقِيمُ الآنَ؟
Where are you staying now? Answer in a full sentence with يُقِيمُ and a place.

مَتَى وَصَلْتَ إِلَى القَاهِرَةِ؟
When did you arrive in Cairo? Answer with the past tense of يَصِلُ.

هَلْ تَمْشِي إِلَى مَحَطَّةِ البِنْزِينِ أَمْ تَأْخُذُ سَيَّارَةَ أُجْرَةٍ؟
Do you walk to the service station or take a taxi? Answer with يَمْشِي or a taxi phrase.

أَيْنَ قِسْمُ الشُّرْطَةِ مِنَ الفُنْدُقِ، عَلَى اليَمِينِ أَمْ عَلَى الشِّمَالِ؟
Where is the police station from the hotel, on the right or the left? Practice يَمِينٌ / يَسَارٌ with مُقَابِلَ.

مَنْ يَمْلَأُ اسْتِمَارَةَ حَجْزِ الفُنْدُقِ، أَنْتَ أَمْ مُوَظَّفُ الِاسْتِقْبَالِ؟
Who fills in the hotel booking form, you or the receptionist? Answer with يَمْلَأُ.

Role-play: you are at the arrival lounge (صَالَةُ الوُصُولِ). A customs officer (ضَابِطُ جَوَازَاتٍ) asks for your passport, then a taxi driver asks where you are going. Play both parts out loud, switching pronouns as you switch roles.

Unit 2 — Residence

هَلِ الشَّقَّةُ مَفْرُوشَةٌ أَمْ فَارِغَةٌ؟
Is the apartment furnished or vacant? Answer with مَفْرُوشَةٌ or فَارِغَةٌ / خَالِيَةٌ.

هَلِ الحَيُّ هَادِئٌ أَمْ مُزْعِجٌ؟
Is the neighborhood quiet or disturbing? Practice the × pair هَادِئٌ × مُزْعِجٌ.

كَمْ غُرْفَةَ نَوْمٍ فِي الشَّقَّةِ، وَهَلْ لَهَا حَمَّامٌ مُسْتَقِلٌّ أَمْ مُشْتَرَكٌ؟
How many bedrooms are in the apartment, and does it have a private or shared bathroom?

مَنْ صَاحِبُ الشَّقَّةِ، وَهَلْ تَعَامَلْتَ مَعَ سِمْسَارٍ؟
Who is the apartment owner, and did you deal with a broker?

Role-play: call a real estate agency (مَكْتَبُ عَقَارَاتٍ) and describe the apartment you want, using at least six words from the Unit 2 table above, then have the broker describe one back to you.
