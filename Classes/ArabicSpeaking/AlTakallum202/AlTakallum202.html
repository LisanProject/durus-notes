Verb Conjugation Drills

يَتَأَلَّمُ and يَشْكُو مِنْ cover complaint of pain (the whole point of a clinic visit), while يَشْتَرِي and يَزِنُ carry the market unit. يَشْكُو is worth extra repetition, since the وpast tense drops the ت where you would expect it.

Speaking Practice

Unit 3 — Health

مِنْ أَيْنَ تَتَأَلَّمُ؟
Where does it hurt? Answer with a body part from the table and يَتَأَلَّمُ.

هَلْ عِنْدَكَ صُدَاعٌ أَمْ رَشْحٌ؟
Do you have a headache or a cold? Answer in a full sentence, not one word.

مُنْذُ مَتَى وَأَنْتَ تَشْكُو مِنَ السُّعَالِ؟
Since when have you been complaining of the cough? Practice يَشْكُو مِنْ in the past tense.

Role-play: you are the patient describing three symptoms to a doctor, who then prescribes a دَوَاء and tells you to take a قُرْص twice a day. Play both parts.

Unit 4 — Shopping

مَاذَا تَشْتَرِي مِنَ الخُضَرِيِّ اليَوْمَ؟
What are you buying from the greengrocer today? List at least three vegetables.

هَلِ اللَّحْمُ طَازَجٌ أَمْ بَائِتٌ؟
Is the meat fresh or stale? Drill the × pair طَازَجٌ × بَائِتٌ.

كَمْ يَزِنُ الجَزَّارُ مِنَ اللَّحْمِ المَفْرُومِ؟
How much minced meat does the butcher weigh out? Practice يَزِنُ with a kilo amount.

Role-play: at the جَزَّار, ask for a specific cut and weight, ask if there is a تَخْفِيض today, and haggle for a moment before agreeing on a price.
