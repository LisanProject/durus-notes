104 Unit 6 — Vacation

Speaking and writing practice for Al-Takallum Elementary Unit 6. Say the speaking items out loud before checking the model answer; any true, grammatically sound answer that uses the target structure counts as correct, the model answer is one example, not the only one.

  1. Speaking: answer in a full sentence. أَيْنَ سَتَقْضِي عُطْلَتَكَ القَادِمَةَ؟ (Where will you spend your next vacation?) Solution
  2. Speaking: describe a photo of the pyramids using two verbs from Unit 6, يُطِلُّ عَلَى and يُشَاهِدُ. Solution
  3. Role-play (speaking, out loud): you are a tour guide. Introduce the Bibliotheca Alexandrina to a group in two or three sentences, then invite them to take photographs. Solution
  4. Conjugate يَسْتَرِيحُ (he rests) for نَحْنُ in the present and past, then use the past-tense form in a sentence about a vacation. Solution
  5. Writing: write four to five sentences describing a trip you took, using the past tense of يُسَافِرُ, يَسْتَمْتِعُ بِـ, and يَلْتَقِطُ صُوَرًا at least once each. Solution
  6. Writing: write a short paragraph, two to three sentences, comparing traveling by plane versus traveling by train, saying which you prefer and why. Solution
