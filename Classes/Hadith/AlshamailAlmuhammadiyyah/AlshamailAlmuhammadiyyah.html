Al-Tirmidhi

Target: Full memorization. The most important book ever written about the person of the Prophet peace be upon him - his appearance, his habits, his character. Reading this text transforms the relationship of the student with the Prophet peace be upon him.

Topics

  1. Chapter 1-5 - Physical Description (Khalq) - The Prophet's peace be upon him seal, hair, complexion, gait, and clothing.
  2. Chapter 6-15 - Food, Drink & Household - What the Prophet peace be upon him ate, drank, and how he lived at home.
  3. Chapter 16-30 - Worship & Prayer - His prayer, his night prayer, his fasting, and his remembrance of Allah.
  4. Chapter 31-56 - Character & Final Illness - His laughter, his speech, his humility, and his passing peace be upon him.
