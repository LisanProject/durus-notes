Al-Bayquni

The foundational poem on the sciences of hadith classification. 34 lines covering the major categories of hadith. Target: memorization and mastery of the terminology.

Topics

  1. Lines 1-10 - Sahih, Hasan, Da'if - The three primary grades; their conditions and sub-types.
  2. Lines 11-20 - Mawquf, Maqtu', Musnad, Muttasil - Categories based on the chain's connection or disconnection.
  3. Lines 21-34 - Shadhdh, Munkar, Mawdu' & More - Problematic categories; the fabricated hadith and its signs.
