Liban's Online Notes

Imam Malik ibn Anas

Target: Full memorization. The oldest organized collection of hadith and fiqh, compiled by the Imam of the People of Madinah. Al-Shafi'i said: "There is no book on the face of the earth after the Book of Allah more correct than the Muwatta'."

Topics

  1. Kitab al-Taharah - Purity; wudu'; what breaks it. The ahadith of Madinan practice.
  2. Kitab al-Salah - Times, call to prayer, imamah, jumu'ah, funeral prayer.
  3. Kitab al-Zakah & al-Sawm - Zakah thresholds; Ramadan; zakat al-fitr.
  4. Kitab al-Hajj - Pilgrimage rituals according to the practice of Madinah.
  5. Kitab al-Buyu' onward - Transactions, marriage, divorce, inheritance, and miscellaneous chapters.
