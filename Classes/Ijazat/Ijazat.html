Ijāzāt

الإجازات

An ijāzah is a written authorization from a teacher to a student, granting permission to teach or transmit what has been studied. The ones listed here are my own received from my teachers after studying the relevant texts under them. Through each chain, I am connected link by link to the scholars who carried this knowledge before us, and ultimately back to the Prophet ﷺ himself.
