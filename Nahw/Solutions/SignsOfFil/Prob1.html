1. قَدْ نَجَحَ الطَّالِبُ

"The student has indeed succeeded." قَدْ enters upon the past tense verb نَجَحَ, conveying confirmation that the action has definitely occurred. Since qad attaches only to verbs, its presence confirms نَجَحَ is a verb.
