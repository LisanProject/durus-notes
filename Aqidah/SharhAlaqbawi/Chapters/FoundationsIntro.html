Chapter 1: Foundations

This chapter covers the opening of the matn: the obligation of knowledge itself, the meaning of the Basmala, the conditions of moral responsibility (taklif), and the overall framework of twenty required attributes that everything else in the book builds on.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

The Khutbah, the Basmala, and the Mukallaf: Why classical books open the way they do, what "Bismillah" actually means grammatically, and the conditions that make a person legally and religiously responsible.

Why Twenty Attributes?: How Allah's perfections can be infinite while only twenty are required knowledge, how the ninety nine names relate back to a much smaller set of attributes, and the four categories the matn actually divides them into.
