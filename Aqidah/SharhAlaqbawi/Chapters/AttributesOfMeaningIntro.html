Chapter 4: The Attributes of Meaning

This chapter covers the seven attributes of meaning (sifat al ma'ani), the real, positive qualities genuinely subsisting in Allah's essence, along with the Seven Demands every one of them must satisfy.

Here is a list of the sections covered in this chapter.

Life, Knowledge, Will, and Power: Hayah, 'Ilm, Iradah, and Qudrah in detail, and how "relevance" (ta'alluq) differs between them.

Hearing, Sight, and Speech: Sam', Basar, and Kalam, and the crucial distinction between Allah's eternal Speech (kalam nafsi) and the created recitation and writing of the Qur'an (kalam lafzi).
