Islamic Notes

Free structured notes on the traditional Islamic sciences, organised by discipline and text. Each discipline follows classical texts in sequence — Arabic matn, translation, commentary, key terms, and cross-references between books.

Where to start If you are new, begin with Ash'ari 'Aqidah — Sharh al-'Aqbawi. It introduces the wajib / mustahil / ja'iz framework that all other texts assume.
